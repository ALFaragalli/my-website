Flying Junior (FJ) Team Racing

In sailing there are two ways to race: Fleet Racing, where it's every boat against every boat. Team Racing where it's three boats against another three boats at a time. This is where you will learn more about Team racing and the difference betweeen fleet and team racing.

Rules Difference

Some rules in team racing either get changed, don't apply, or new ones get added. Here are the rules that gets affected.

  1. Rule 18.2 is changed.
  2. Rule 18.4 does not apply.
  3. Rule 20.4 is changed.
  4. Rule 23.1 is changed
  5. Rule 23.3 gets added
  6. Rule 41 gets changed
  7. Rule 45 does not apply
